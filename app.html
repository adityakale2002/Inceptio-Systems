What Does Intimus APP Delivers..



Intimus Sales App

The Intimus Sales App provides sales staff with comprehensive customer information, including details of in-house guests, outstanding balances, sales performance, and call history. It also allows them to manage their appointment diaries online. Managers can monitor the performance and schedules of their sales team, offering support when needed, ensuring seamless sales operations.

Intimus HK App

Intimus HK App allows to assign staff to clean rooms, update a checklist template, create a service schedule and update completion of activities. A supervisor is then able to carry out inspection, where necessary get the process re-done and certify the process as completed.

Intimus Tracker

Intimus Tracker is an app that tracks operational interactions between staff & departments. It can be used in various areas such as Guest Complaints tracking, Sales Calls, Meetings or any type of internal communication that requires multi-level visibility across the organization.



Intimus Messaging App

Intimus Messaging is an app that tracks operational interactions between staff & departments. It can be used in various areas such as Guest Complaints tracking, Sales Calls, Meetings or any type of internal communication that requires multi-level visibility across the organization. Intimus Messaging app allows the property to send mass SMS messages and mailers to multiple recipients, effortlessly from Intimus. These can be used for internal & external communications and can be used for promotions, information sharing as well as reservation confirmations.
